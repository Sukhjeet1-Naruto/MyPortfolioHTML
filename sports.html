Football



I still remember those days when I used to play soccer quite often during my childhood. It was the world-cup season of 2006. The game was hosted by Germany who won the right to host the event in July 2000. There was a huge excitement of the game all over my place. Fortunately, it was holiday time. I used to watch the game in the big screen and try to copy their styles of playing and when I go for playing football, me and my friends used to tag name of the famous players. I still remember the best match that I have ever seen in my life when, "Zidane" single handedly won two matches against spain and brazil(which had likes of Ronaldo, Kaka and Ronaldinho the best players in the world) and saved his best for this tournament quaterfinal vs brazil in 2006 FIFA WORLD CUP.



This is how I started playing soccor and being passionate soccer fan.





In school day, A senior football player taught us the basic skills. We improved a lot in a short period of time and played & won few matches too at intra school level. Moreover, I used to play as a team memeber of my college football team and learned a lot of new skills for my mates and won most of our college matches.

It gives me inspiration like never giving up attitude, team spirit, getting excellence through hard word and dedication. I love this sport because, it is a most popular sport in the world and it used to feel good when discussing with friends about the game. It is a beautiful game where two teams fights with their own strategy and try to execute their plans to succeed just like we fight hard in life to succeed.
